Overview

The Critspace framework defines the basic structure of a panel (a box with certain visual properties and control areas) but doesn't say anything about what type of content is displayed by the panel or how users can interact with that content. This task is deferred to panel extensions. Here we'll take a look at how to implement a panel extension. To do this, we'll walk through the process of creating a simple panel extension, the ImagePanel, that will dispaly (you guessed it), an image.
